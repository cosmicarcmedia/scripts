4 Live Oak FAQs

Answer these to camera. Becomes a "San Angelo asked, Live Oak answered" shorts series. Split between Mason and Stacey.

Hormones / TRT (men)

  • What is testosterone replacement therapy in plain English?
  • Signs of low T that guys brush off as "just getting older"?
  • Myth: isn't TRT just for bodybuilders or cheating? What's the real story?
  • Will it make me bald, infertile, or aggressive?
  • What age should a guy actually start paying attention to his hormones?
  • Do you have to be on it forever?

Weight Loss / GLP-1

  • What are GLP-1 medications and how do they actually work?
  • Myth: is it "the easy way out"? What do you tell people who say that?
  • Who's a good candidate, and who isn't?
  • Difference between doing this with a provider vs buying stuff online?
  • Is there an off-ramp or do you take it forever?

Longevity / Labs / Performance

Ties straight to the testing stations behind you.

  • Why does Live Oak put so much focus on bloodwork?
  • Difference between "normal" lab ranges and "optimal"?
  • What are VO2 max and RMR, and why should the average person care?
  • Does "longevity" mean living longer, or living better?
  • One thing most people could do today to add healthy years?

Women's Health

  • Is hormone optimization just a men's thing? What about women?
  • What symptoms should women not just write off?
  • How does this connect to perimenopause and menopause?

Why Live Oak

  • What makes Live Oak different from a regular doctor's visit?
  • Walk me through what happens when someone becomes a patient.
  • Do you have to live in San Angelo to work with you? (telehealth hook)
  • The #1 question you wish more people asked?
Keep it compliant Educational only. No promising specific results, no diagnosing on camera. End the medical ones with "talk to a provider about what's right for you." Mason's funny voice is fine, just keep the claims clean.
